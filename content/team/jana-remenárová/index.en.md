Jana has been working in non-profit organizations supporting people with migration experience for more than 15 years. During this time, she has coordinated educational programmes, community and integration projects, as well as RefuFest, one of the Czech Republic’s largest intercultural festivals. Through these activities, she has helped create space for people from different backgrounds to meet, share their experiences, and actively participate in Czech society.

She believes that meaningful integration starts with dialogue, mutual understanding, and opportunities for people to actively participate in the communities they live in. For Jana, being part of MigAct is an opportunity to continue supporting these values and working with people who share the same vision of an open and inclusive society.
