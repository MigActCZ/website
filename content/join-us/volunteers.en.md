## Join as a Volunteer
Become part of our growing network of active residents helping to make Prague more inclusive.  
Volunteers help us organize community gatherings, support during events, take photos, facilitate workshops, translate materials, or contribute as experts in their field.  
Your time and skills can make a real difference — and you’ll meet great people along the way. Get in touch to learn more about current opportunities.

- Organizing
- Event support
- Photography
- Workshop facilitation
- Translation
- Expert input
