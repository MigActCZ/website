This project responds to the growing need for systematic support of civic and community participation among residents with migration backgrounds in the Prague 3 district. It builds on the ongoing pilot cooperation between MigAct, z.s. and the local government in the areas of strengthening neighborly ties, community life, and civic engagement. The goal of the project is to map the activities, needs, and motivations of residents with migration backgrounds in Prague 3, and subsequently support their involvement in the local community and civil society. The project creates a safe space for open dialogue between local residents, institutions, and community stakeholders. Through participatory and community meetings, it facilitates the sharing of experiences, collaborative problem-solving, and the building of mutual trust.
