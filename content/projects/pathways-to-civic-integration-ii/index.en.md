This project follows up on the [pilot cooperation](https://migact.net/projects/pathways-to-civic-integration-of-residents-with-migrant-backgrounds/) between MigAct, Endowment Fund Agora 7 and the Prague 7 Municipal District in 2025, which resulted in a [new publication](https://migact.net/ac/civic-integration-of-migrant-residents-in-prague-7/good-practices-and-local-insights-on-civic-integration-of-migrant-residents-in-prague-7_eng.pdf) and a working group of active neighbors. This year, we are focusing on providing methodological support to this group, strengthening the role of "gatekeepers"—guides to civic and community life—and organizing networking events. By planning activities together and sharing experiences, we are creating an open space in Prague 7 for the active citizenship of all residents.
