We live in a world that is becoming increasingly fluid, unpredictable, and complex, where democracy is under continuous threat and the topic of migration is increasingly contentious. And yet, it is also a world which remains intensely interconnected, where people constantly move, relocate, and build new homes and futures.

In 2025, we doubled our efforts to address these challenges by supporting local democracy and creating inclusive spaces for genuine human connection and joint action. Guided by our Theory of Change, we expanded our programs, deepened our co-creation efforts, and worked closer with Prague municipalities and grassroots communities.

This collective experience has unlocked new opportunities and ideas, demonstrating that trust and human connection are absolutely vital for breaking down prejudice, supporting democracy and belonging at its very foundations.

As always, none of this would have been possible without the people who worked alongside us—those who co-created, challenged us and themselves, joined our initiatives, and opened themselves up to new connections.

Thank you for standing with us. In a world constantly in motion, we look forward to rooting our community in trust, belonging, and shared democracy throughout the coming year.

MigAct team
