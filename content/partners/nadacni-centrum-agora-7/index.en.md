The Agora 7 Foundation is located in Prague 7 Holešovice. We care about improving the quality of life in senior age and across generations and support the socially disadvantaged, living on the margins of society. Seniors deserve our attention in particular, for whom we operate the Přístav 7 educational and cultural center near Marina Holešovice together with Elpida.
