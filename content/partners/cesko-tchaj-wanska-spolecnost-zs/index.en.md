Czech Taiwanese Association is a non-profit organization, aiming to provide mutual assistance between Taiwanese individuals and families in the Czech Republic, and to further build up the connections among Taiwanese. In addition, the association gathered the government resources to build the first Mandarin school in the Czech Republic,  and organizes cultural events.
