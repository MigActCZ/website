We have become accustomed to the global interconnectedness of the world. But the world is held together by relationships at the local level. At Spořka, we help create stronger neighborhoods and we tell you about it here. A person doesn't have to be rich, or an influential politician or official, to change the place where they live for the better. But they must be willing to talk to their neighbors over the fence, and take an interest in their surroundings.
