  Connect with locals, civic initiatives, NGOs, and government institutions. Take the time to listen, reflect, and gain inspiration. Strengthen your network, exchange knowledge and ideas, and support one another.

- {{<activity-card learning_hubs book-open>}}

  Join our workshops, trainings, and events designed to support your civic engagement. We also offer tailored programs for NGOs, local institutions, and businesses looking to build stronger, more inclusive practices.
  {{</activity-card>}}
- {{<activity-card mentoring message-circle>}}

  Build your skills and confidence through personalized mentoring and peer-to-peer support. Learn how to engage in community projects, explore volunteering, understand voting rights, or start an association of your own.
  {{</activity-card>}}
- {{<activity-card civic_incubators calendar>}}
  ## Civic Incubators
  We bring residents—including those with migration backgrounds—together with local institutions and community groups to transform grassroots ideas into initiatives that bring improvements to everyday life in the neighborhood.
