## Our Story and Values
MigAct builds on the long-standing work of the organization InBáze with migrants in Prague. As an independent association, it was officially established in October 2023.

- {{<icon handshake rounded>}}
  ### Cooperation & Equity
  Cooperation and equity are fundamental to MigAct’s activities, driving us to work transparently, constructively, and respectfully with our community and partners to achieve our shared vision. We define equity as the active pursuit of fairness and justice, focusing on dismantling the barriers that limit access and opportunities for all. By fostering inclusive systems and upholding the universal principles of human rights and democracy, we drive positive change while firmly rejecting intolerance, discrimination, and any harmful ideologies that seek to undermine our society.
- {{<icon scale rounded>}}
  ### Responsibility & Active Participation
  At MigAct, we believe that democracy is a practice rather than just a concept, which is why we commit to active participation and responsibility. We strive to create a space where everyone feels invited to contribute to the common good, turning civic involvement into a powerful force for social development and belonging. This commitment is guided by a fundamental sense of responsibility, requiring that all participation be rooted in informed choices and personal accountability. This dedication extends to our organization, where we work with integrity and transparency to ensure that our collective actions drive meaningful and responsible change.
- {{<icon users rounded>}}
  ### Intercultural Sensitivity
  Intercultural sensitivity is vital to MigAct’s identity, reflecting our commitment to navigating the complexities of a diverse society with empathy and awareness. We recognize that true inclusion and belonging requires more than just the presence of different backgrounds; it demands a deep respect for the diverse perspectives, traditions, and lived experiences that each individual brings.
