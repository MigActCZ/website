How can we talk about important topics in a way that builds understanding rather than division? That’s exactly what the Democracy Dialogues method is about—finding connection with others and with ourselves. It uses facilitated conversations in small groups and specially designed cards that help guide the discussion, encourage listening, and open up a broader understanding of things without the need to seek solutions.

Would you like to experience a meaningful dialogue facilitated using the DEMOS method? Our facilitators have been trained in this approach—and now they are inviting you to their first public event.

- **A safe space for sharing opinions** 
- **Guided dialogue in small groups** 
- **Card-based conversations that go deeper**
- **Openness, respect, and active listening**

Come and try a different way of talking about the things that matter. Let’s talk—really talk_._ We look forward to seeing you!

The project “Practising Dialogue: Civic Innovation in Diverse Urban Communities” was funded by the Civic Innovation Fund (CIF), a unique European pooled fund of THE CIVICS Innovation Hub. CIF is supported by the Robert Bosch Stiftung.
