The art of dialogue. Join us on April 29!

How can we talk about important topics in a way that builds understanding rather than division?

That’s exactly what the Democracy Dialogues method is about.

It uses facilitated conversations in small groups and specially designed cards that help guide the discussion, encourage listening, and open up different perspectives.

Would you like to experience a meaningful dialogue facilitated using the DEMOS method?

<!--more-->

Our facilitators have been trained in this approach — and now they are inviting you to their first public event.

- Safe space for sharing opinions
- Guided dialogue in small groups
- Card-based conversations that go deeper
- Openness, respect, and active listening

Come and try a different way of talking about the things that matter.

Let’s talk — really talk.
