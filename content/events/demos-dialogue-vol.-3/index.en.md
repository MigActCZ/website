How can we talk about challenging topics in ways that build understanding instead of division?

Join us for the third session of the DEMOS Dialogue series, based on the methodology by HERMES

Together, we will create a safe space for open conversations, active listening, and the exchange of different perspectives.

Whether you have attended previous sessions or this is your first DEMOS Dialogue, you are warmly welcome. No prior experience is needed—just curiosity, openness, and a willingness to engage in respectful dialogue.

 For more info: contact@migact.net

The project “Practising Dialogue: Civic Innovation in Diverse Urban Communities” was funded by the Civic Innovation Fund (CIF), a unique European pooled fund of THE CIVICS Innovation Hub. The Civic Innovation Fund is supported by the Robert Bosch Stiftung.
